# Stakeholder Communication Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure consistent, timely, and appropriate communication with all stakeholders throughout the project lifecycle. Owners are indicated in parentheses.

---

## Project Initiation

- [ ] Identify all stakeholder groups and assign a Stakeholder Group Representative for each (Project Manager)
- [ ] Document stakeholder names, roles, contact information, and communication preferences (Project Manager)
- [ ] Share the project charter / one-pager with all relevant stakeholders (Project Manager + Project Sponsor)
- [ ] Confirm each stakeholder's level of involvement and decision rights (Project Manager)
- [ ] Schedule kickoff meeting and send invitations to all stakeholders (Project Manager)

---

## Ongoing Delivery

- [ ] Send weekly status update to stakeholders including progress, risks, and upcoming milestones (Project Manager)
- [ ] Ensure Stakeholder Group Representatives attend or receive summaries of sprint reviews (Project Manager + Stakeholder Group Representative)
- [ ] Log all decisions and share the decision log with stakeholders when updated (Project Manager)
- [ ] Escalate blockers that affect stakeholders to the Project Sponsor promptly (Project Manager)
- [ ] Collect stakeholder feedback on deliverables at agreed review checkpoints (Stakeholder Group Representative)
- [ ] Communicate any scope, timeline, or budget changes to all stakeholders before they take effect (Project Manager + Project Sponsor)

---

## Change Events

- [ ] Notify all impacted stakeholders of upcoming changes at least one sprint/cycle in advance (Change Manager)
- [ ] Provide a change impact summary describing what is changing, why, and what action stakeholders need to take (Change Manager)
- [ ] Coordinate with Stakeholder Group Representatives to relay change communications within their groups (Change Manager)
- [ ] Confirm receipt and understanding of change communications with each stakeholder group (Stakeholder Group Representative)
- [ ] Make training or support materials available before changes go live (Change Manager)

---

## Milestone Reviews and Go/No-Go Decisions

- [ ] Schedule milestone review meetings with sufficient lead time (Project Manager)
- [ ] Distribute review materials (status, metrics, risks) to the Project Sponsor and stakeholders 48 hours in advance (Project Manager)
- [ ] Capture and communicate go/no-go decisions and rationale to all stakeholders (Project Manager + Project Sponsor)
- [ ] Follow up on any open action items from milestone reviews (Project Manager)

---

## Project Close

- [ ] Notify all stakeholders of project completion and any final deliverables (Project Manager)
- [ ] Share retrospective findings and improvement actions with interested stakeholders (Project Manager + Process Owner)
- [ ] Archive project communication records per OctoAcme documentation standards (Project Manager)
- [ ] Conduct a brief stakeholder satisfaction check and capture feedback (Project Manager)
